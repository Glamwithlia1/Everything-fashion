Frequently Asked Questions

Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions about our fashion collections, shipping, and policies. Can’t find what you need? Contact us here.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Delivery times vary depending on your location. You can see estimated shipping times at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, Apple Pay, and other secure payment options. All transactions are encrypted for safety.

What is your return policy?

You can return items within 30 days of delivery, provided they are unworn and in original packaging. For more info, visit our Returns Page.

How do I know my size?

We offer a detailed Size Guide with measurements to help you choose the right fit. If you’re in between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

Do you restock sold-out items?

Some bestsellers are restocked, while others are limited drops. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us on Instagram to stay updated on restocks.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you ll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also log in to your account and view order status.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer seasonal sales and exclusive discounts to newsletter subscribers. Join our mailing list to never miss a deal.
