About Glam with Lia

Everything Fashion Hub

Glam with Lia is your one-stop fashion destination — where style meets confidence. We specialize in trendy dresses, elegant accessories, classy heels & shoes, and glamorous hair pieces. Our goal is to empower women and fashion lovers everywhere to express their beauty, confidence, and creativity through their unique sense of style.

From timeless elegance to modern chic, we curate high-quality, affordable pieces that make every outfit stand out. Whether you're dressing for a casual day, a night out, or a special occasion, Glam with Lia helps you stay effortlessly fashionable.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted fashion hub known for redefining beauty, style, and confidence across Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality fashion essentials — from dresses and accessories to hair and shoes — that inspire confidence and self-expression.

Our Promise

We bring you carefully curated collections that blend comfort, elegance, and glamour — because every woman deserves to feel beautiful.

Meet Lia

Founder & Creative Director

Lia is a passionate fashion lover with a bold vision — to redefine everyday style and make confidence wearable. Her love for design and eye for detail inspired the creation of Glam with Lia, a brand built to help people look and feel their best no matter the occasion.

Through Glam with Lia, she combines creativity and authenticity, offering affordable luxury that celebrates individuality, elegance, and self-expression. Every collection reflects her belief that fashion is not just about what you wear — it’s about how it makes you feel.
